Welcome!

The Community School of West Seattle(CSWS) is a unique and special place. Our strong committment to anti-bias practices and an 'Everyone is Welcome' policy gives our school a warm and inviting feel. Children are the focus here with an emphasis on supporting the family. Being here is really the best way to get a true sense of who we are. We welcome you to schedule a visit. We hope the information here answers many of your questions but if not please feel free to email or call. The early years, birth to eight years old, are a time of intense discovery. Play is the vital work of childhood as young children learn by doing. We believe the more opportunities children have for hands on, sensory-based experiences, the more they learn about their world. Our multi-age approach allows children to work with, learn from, and support each other.
